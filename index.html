About Clearframe

Clearframe Music Publishing is a Connecticut-based music publishing company dedicated to serving professional independent artists and songwriters who have been underrepresented by traditional publishing infrastructure.

Founded in 2025, Clearframe emerged from seven years of hosting songwriter workshops throughout New England, where a consistent challenge became clear: highly talented regional artists lack access to professional publishing services that can properly register, protect, and monetize their original compositions.

Our Mission

We bridge the gap between exceptional independent artistry and professional music rights management, providing accessible publishing services that enable songwriters to generate revenue from performance royalties, mechanical royalties, and synchronization licensing opportunities.

What Makes Us Different

Traditional publishing companies focus on commercially proven artists with existing industry connections. Clearframe serves the underrepresented middle tier‚Äîprofessional-quality independent artists who deserve proper representation but don't fit the major label model.

We combine traditional artist development with contemporary fast-turnaround licensing solutions, addressing both long-term career building and immediate content creator needs.
